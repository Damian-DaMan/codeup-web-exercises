New York Style Pizza from Your Oven

 

Bake within 60 minutes of purchase or refrigerate. If refrigerated, remove 60 minutes prior to baking for crust to rise. Always bake pizzas within 24 hours of purchase.

 

Step 1 - Get ready

Preheat oven to 425°F. Remove plastic wrap and leave XLNY pizza on tray.

Step 2 - Let your oven do the work

Bake XLNY pizza on center oven rack for approximately 12 to 18 minutes (varies by oven). Remove pizza from oven when the crust is golden brown.

Notice any bubbles appearing? Pop them with a fork.

Step 3 - Slice it up!

Let pizza sit for 5 minutes before cutting. Enjoy the foldable New York Style crust!

Pizza Tips & Tricks

CRISPIER CRUST

After 10 to 12 minutes, slide pizza off tray directly onto oven rack. Ready when golden brown. Excludes Pan Pizza!

POP BUBBLES

If bubbles form while baking, carefully pop with a fork. Bubbles tend to make toppings slide.

RACK IT

Center rack = Original, Pan, Stuffed or Gluten Free. Lower Rack = Thin or XLNY (at least 5 inches from bottom).

ROTATE

Does your oven heat unevenly? Rotating your pizza 180 degrees every 5 to 7 minutes will take care of that.

BAKING 2 PIZZAS

Place one on lower rack left. The other on upper rack right. Rotate half-way through. Crispiness all around!
